**Hallo, Welt!**

I've been studying operads more and more, and I've worked with an other phd student on the Koszul dual of the Com² Operad.
It was very fun, she introduced me to the methods for Com, and then we did it again with Com², and saw that the Koszul dual of Com was Lie, and of Com² was Lie².
We're working on the following paper : Dotsenko and Khoroshkin - Character formulas for the operad of two compatible brackets and for the bihamiltonian operad, in order to get to the Fröbenius characteristic of Com².
For now, I'll have to dive further in this Fröbenius characteristic, it requires some representation theory, and afterwards I guess we'll try and compute it for Com².
I'm looking forward to doing so, it is indeed super fun.

I also started to write some notes on obsidian, and will make a paper about it soon.
